Pixels from my journal

Hey! I am Himaja.

A born-and-raised Indian girl with nothing more than passion, a laptop, and a dream. Since childhood colors, fashion and creativity have attracted me. I created paintings and hand-made decorative and useful things for home and for family and friends. I like to keep myself busy with creating things and experiences in my spare time. I spend my free hours with canvas and colors, working out in gym, dancing, sports and sometimes trying out new recipes in the kitchen. Traveling around the world is on my bucket list since long.

I started this blog many iterations ago because I felt need to reach out to more people with what I have to offer. It took me some time to come up with this idea of making TheDesignPixel and getting it to the real world. I would love it if you guys can be part of it in any way!
