The San Francisco thing!

When in San Francisco: I am a travelholic person and San Francisco was on my bucket list. Since my sister stays in SF I got an opportunity to visit the city for 2 months. Not a long period but within that time I fell for the city. Two months in the city easily convinced me, why it is a dreamland for coders and engineers around the world. There were the days when I loved going to Golden gate bridge for morning runs and if I still get the chance I would go. The bridge is 1.6 km long and I am happy that I could finish the 3.2 km run in one go. (One side to other side and coming back)In those few days what I noticed was there were very few people who come there early morning for a run but I could spot many cyclists riding the bicycles. And the thing amazed me was whenever I am passing by them they all cheer me by showing thumbs up. This is one of the examples why I fell for the city! I love the way they appreciate you or any other human being for what they are doing no matter how big or small it is.

The architecture: San Francisco is known worldwide for its particularly eclectic mix of Victorian and modern architecture. Bay windows were identified as a defining characteristic of San Francisco architecture. Icons of San Francisco architecture include the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, Coit Tower, the Palace of Fine Arts, Painted ladies, Lombard Street, Alamo Square, Fort Point, and Chinatown. I always loved walking around the city. For our weekly grocery shopping, I always walked down to the nearest supermarket and while coming back I used to take new routes so that I could see the new neighborhood.
